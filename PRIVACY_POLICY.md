# Privacy Policy — IronLore

Effective date: April 29, 2026

IronLore collects and uses information to provide core app features such as accounts, workout tracking, nutrition logging, progress tracking, and the AI Coach feature.

## Information we collect

- Account information: email address
- Health & fitness data you enter in the app: workouts, training stats, body weight, nutrition logs, goals
- AI Coach messages: text you choose to send to the Coach feature

## How we use your information

- To operate the app features you use
- To authenticate your account and keep your data saved to your profile
- To provide the AI Coach feature and respond to your messages
- To improve reliability and prevent abuse (for example, rate limiting)

## Sharing

We may share data with service providers that help us run the app (for example: backend hosting/database and AI processing providers). We do not sell your personal data.

## Tracking

We do not track you across third‑party apps or websites for advertising.

## Data retention

We retain your information as long as needed to provide the service, unless you request deletion.
