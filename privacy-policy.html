IRONLORE

Privacy Policy — Last updated April 28, 2026

⚔ IronLore does not sell your data. We do not run ads. We never will.

Who We Are

IronLore is a fitness tracking application developed by Dillon Reynolds. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the IronLore app.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's features:

  • Account information — email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account
  • Fitness data — workouts, sets, reps, weights, body weight logs, and nutrition entries you choose to log
  • Supplement data — supplement names, dosages, and logging history you enter
  • Usage data — app activity such as streak counts and last login date
  • AI Coach conversations — messages sent to the AI coach to generate responses

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide app functionality:

  • To sync your fitness data across devices
  • To power the AI coach with context about your goals and history
  • To calculate streaks, progress, and achievements
  • To authenticate your account securely

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than operating the app.

AI Coach

The IronLore AI Coach is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you send a message to your coach, your message and relevant fitness context (your class, goals, and character name) are sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. We do not store your full conversation history on our servers — conversations are session-based.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use row-level security to ensure your data is only accessible to you.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Supabase — for secure data storage and authentication
  • Anthropic — for AI coach message processing
  • Open Food Facts — barcode and food search queries (no personal data sent)

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us, which will permanently delete all associated data within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

IronLore is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your fitness data

Security

We take security seriously. Your password is never stored in plain text. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We use Supabase's row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of IronLore after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
